MOON

Our moon, Earth's sole satellite, hangs about 238,000 miles away, influencing tides, stabilizing our climate, and dazzling us with its ever-changing phases. It's a rocky world, scarred by ancient impacts, with a mysterious dark side we're still exploring. Humans have even left their footprints there!

DUCK

The most common meaning is a type of waterfowl. Ducks are small to medium-sized birds with webbed feet, short necks, and broad bills. They come in a variety of colors and patterns, and are found all over the world. Ducks are popular as pets and game birds, and their meat and eggs are eaten in many cultures.

BEACH & HORSES

Horses thunder along the wet sand, manes flying against the salty breeze. Waves crash, echoing their gallop, as sun glints off their powerful muscles. Freedom reigns in this wild dance of beach and beast.
