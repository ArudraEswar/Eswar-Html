About Us

Fantasy is a genre of literature that involves elements of the supernatural, the fantastic, or the imaginative. Fantasy stories often take place in a world that is different from our own, where magic, mythical creatures, or other extraordinary phenomena exist. Fantasy can also be a form of escapism, where the reader can explore alternative realities or experience wonder and adventure.

Fantasy has a long and rich history, dating back to ancient myths and folklore from various cultures. Some of the earliest examples of fantasy literature are the epic poems of Homer, such as the Iliad and the Odyssey, which feature gods, monsters, and heroes in ancient Greece. Other ancient works that influenced fantasy include the Indian epics of Ramayana and Mahabharata, the Persian tales of One Thousand and One Nights, and the Norse sagas of Odin and Thor.
