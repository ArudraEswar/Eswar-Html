Introduction

SHAREit is a file sharing and transfer app that works without internet or Bluetooth connection. It is free, fast, and supports all types of files and cross-platform transferring. It also offers other features such as downloader, file manager, phone cleaner, music player, and online video.

History

SHAREit was launched in 2012 by Lenovo as part of its Doit apps suite. It quickly became popular among users who wanted to share files without using cables or data. In 2015, SHAREit spun off from Lenovo and became an independent company. Since then, it has expanded its services to more than 200 countries and regions, with over 1.8 billion users worldwide.

Features

SHAREit has many features that make it more than just a file sharing app. Here are some of them:

File Sharing

SHAREit allows you to share any type of file, such as photos, videos, music, documents, apps, and more. You can share files with up to five devices at the same time, with speeds up to 200 times faster than Bluetooth. You can also share files across different platforms, such as Android, iOS, Windows, Mac, and Linux.

Downloader

SHAREit has a built-in downloader that lets you download videos, music, wallpapers, stickers, and gifs from various sources. You can also download online videos from popular platforms like YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. You can watch the downloaded videos offline or share them with your friends.

File Manager

SHAREit has a file manager that helps you organize your files on your device. You can view, move, copy, delete, rename, and compress your files with ease. You can also access your files on cloud storage services like Google Drive and Dropbox. You can also backup your files to your PC or another device using SHAREit.

Phone Cleaner

SHAREit has a phone cleaner that helps you optimize your device performance. You can scan and remove junk files, cache files, duplicate files, and large files that take up space on your device. You can also boost your device speed by closing background apps and freeing up memory.

Music Player

SHAREit has a music player that lets you enjoy your favorite songs offline or online. You can create playlists, adjust the equalizer, and discover new music from various genres and artists. You can also share your music with your friends using SHAREit.

Online Video

SHAREit has an online video feature that lets you watch thousands of HD videos for free. You can choose from different categories like movies, TV shows, sports, news, and more. You can also stream live TV channels from around the world. You can also comment and interact with other users while watching videos.
